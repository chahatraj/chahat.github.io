An NLP Researcher!

I am a third-year Ph.D. student, majoring in Computer Science at George Mason University. I'm advised by Prof. Ziwei Zhu and Prof. Antonios Anastasopoulos. I also collaborate closely with Prof. Aylin Caliskan at the University of Washington.

I specialize in sociocultural biases, ethics, and fairness in multimodal and multilingual NLP. Currently, I am working towards understanding how biases perpetuate and reinforce in (large) vision-language models, and exploring ways to mitigate these. I draw on concepts from sociology and psychology to add depth and perspective to my research.
